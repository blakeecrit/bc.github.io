Meatloaf
- 2 lb. ground beef
- 1 pkg Lipton Onion Soup mix
- 1 sleeve saltine crackers, crushed
- 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
- 3/4 cup ketchup
- 1/2 cup water
- dash nutmeg
- 3 Tbl brown sugar

Mix ingredients together and shape into loaf, putting extra ketchup on top, if desired. Bake at 350 degrees for 60 to 90 minutes.
